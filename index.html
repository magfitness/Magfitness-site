Common Questions

Before you book.

If anything else is on your mind, the consult is the right place to ask. These are the ones that come up most often.

Is this for beginners?

Yes. Programs MF-001 and MF-003 are specifically built for people who are new or returning after a long break. The four-pillar system scales from first-week beginner to advanced lifter — what changes is the application, not the framework.

Do I need a gym?

No. MF-003 (Home Foundations) is built around minimal equipment, and the other programs can be adapted if you're training at home. Bring this up on the consult and we'll structure your plan around what you actually have access to.

How long are the programs?

Programs run in structured blocks rather than fixed durations, because real progress is measured in months and quarters, not weeks. Most clients commit to a minimum of three months to see meaningful change. We discuss the right structure for your goal on the call.

What does coaching cost?

Pricing depends on which program suits you and the level of support you want. I prefer to discuss this on the consult so the recommendation is grounded in your situation, not a generic price tier. Payment is via EFT or Bitcoin.

Do I need supplements?

No. Supplements are a small lever in a much larger system. The four pillars produce 95% of results before you ever consider supplementation. If anything is worth adding for your specific case, we'll discuss it.

I'm female — is this for me?

Yes. The system is built around physiology, training response, and goal — not gender. Female-focused programming and considerations are integrated into how we'd structure your plan, and discussed in detail on the consult.

What if I'm not based in Cape Town?

All coaching is delivered online. Most current and incoming clients are across South Africa, with weekly calls handled via Google Meet or WhatsApp video. International clients are accommodated where time zones align.
